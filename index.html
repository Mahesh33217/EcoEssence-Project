About Our Product

Our herbal shampoo powder is a blend of time-tested Indian herbs like Amla, Shikakai, Neem, and Hibiscus. It cleanses gently without stripping natural oils, improves scalp health, and adds natural shine. Packaged in recyclable material. Suitable for all hair types and formulated to work well even with hard water.

Key Ingredients & Benefits

Amla

Strengthens roots, promotes hair growth, rich in vitamin C.

Shikakai

Natural cleanser, reduces dandruff and frizz.

Neem

Antiseptic properties, soothes itchy scalp.

Hibiscus

Nourishes hair, adds shine and softness.

How to Use

1
Prepare paste: Mix 2 tablespoons of powder with water or aloe vera to form a smooth paste.
2
Apply: Massage gently into scalp and distribute through hair.
3
Wait: Leave for 3-7 minutes depending on preference.
4
Rinse: Rinse thoroughly and air-dry or use minimal heat.
